Our Story

AfroFlame Kitchen celebrates South Africa's rich food heritage. Inspired by townshit culture, family gathering, braai traditions, and the spirit of Ubuntu, we bring authentic flavours to every play. Our mission is to share the warmth, diversity, and vibrant taste of Mzansi with every customer.
